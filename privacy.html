Privacy Policy

This policy describes how Astra Gold LLC collects, uses, and protects your personal information in accordance with applicable Washington State and federal law.

Effective Date: January 1, 2025  ·  Last Updated: April 2025  ·  Operator: Astra Gold LLC, Renton, WA

1. Who We Are

Astra Gold LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") is a licensed motor carrier and auto transport company operating under USDOT 4289669 and MC-1668866, headquartered in Renton, Washington. This Privacy Policy applies to information collected through our website at astragoldllc.com and through direct communications with our company.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide to us, including:

  • Name, phone number, and email address submitted via contact or application forms
  • Professional information such as CDL status, years of experience, and truck type (for owner-operator applicants)
  • Messages, inquiries, or any other content you send us directly

We may also automatically collect limited technical data when you visit our website, including:

  • IP address and general geographic location (city/state level)
  • Browser type and device type
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • Referring website or search engine

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect only for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Responding to inquiries and applications submitted through our website
  • Evaluating owner-operator applicants for carrier partnerships
  • Communicating with brokers and shippers regarding loads and carrier services
  • Improving the content and functionality of our website
  • Complying with applicable federal and Washington State legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Washington State Privacy Rights

Washington residents have rights under the Washington My Health MY Data Act (MHMD) and general Washington consumer protection law. As a Washington-based company, we are committed to the following:

  • Right to Know: You may request information about what personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Delete: You may request that we delete your personal information, subject to certain legal exceptions.
  • Right to Correct: You may request correction of inaccurate personal data we hold.
  • No Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at humanresources@astragoldllc.com. We will respond within 45 days as required by Washington law.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law. Application data from owner-operator candidates who are not hired is retained for no more than 12 months unless you request earlier deletion.

6. Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Formspree — processes form submissions on our behalf. Their privacy policy is available at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.
  • Cloudflare — provides content delivery and security services. Their privacy policy is available at cloudflare.com/privacypolicy.
  • Google Fonts — used to load web fonts. Google may log font requests per their privacy policy.

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising pixels, or behavioral analytics tools on this website.

7. Cookies

Our website does not use tracking cookies or persistent cookies. Any session-level cookies set by our hosting infrastructure are strictly technical and are not used to identify or track individual users across sessions or websites.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are intended for adults and are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information to us, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

9. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage method is 100% secure. We encourage you to use caution when transmitting personal information online.

10. Interstate Commerce & FMCSA

As a federally licensed motor carrier, Astra Gold LLC is subject to regulations of the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA) and the U.S. Department of Transportation. Certain information related to our operations — including our USDOT number, MC authority, and insurance filings — is publicly available through the FMCSA SAFER system as required by federal law.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of our website after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
