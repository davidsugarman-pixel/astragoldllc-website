Qualifications

What We Require

We work with experienced operators. If you've hauled cars before and you run a clean operation, we want to talk.

  • 01 Valid CDL-A with clean MVR (no DUI, no serious violations in the past 3 years)
  • 02 Verifiable car hauling experience — open or enclosed, multi-car preferred
  • 03 DOT-compliant car hauler in good working condition (2+ car capacity)
  • 04 Active ELD device installed and operational
  • 05 Physical damage and non-trucking liability insurance (or ability to obtain)
  • 06 US work authorization — W-9 for 1099 independent contractor classification
  • 07 Reliable communication — you pick up, you respond, you deliver updates
